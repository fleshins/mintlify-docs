This release delivers **more forgiving CSV timestamp parsing**, **fewer import failures**, **more consistent AI evaluations**, and **behind-the-scenes security and maintenance updates**.

Below are the most important changes in this release (not every internal tweak, just the ones we think deliver the most value).

---

## 📥 **More forgiving CSV imports**

- **Broader timestamp support:** CSV uploads now accept more timestamp formats, including shorter timezone formats that were previously rejected — Fewer valid files fail during import, reducing manual cleanup and rework.

## 🤖 **More consistent AI evaluations**

- **Improved evaluation completion:** Fixed an issue that could prevent some AI-powered evaluations from completing successfully — You should see fewer failed runs and more consistent analysis results.

## 🛡️ **Behind-the-scenes maintenance**

- **Security and maintenance update:** Included a small security and maintenance update to keep the product dependable behind the scenes — Improves ongoing dependability with no customer action required.

Thanks for using Brixo! We hope these updates make your day-to-day workflows smoother, faster, and more intuitive.
