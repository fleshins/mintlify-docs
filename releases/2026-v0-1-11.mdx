This release delivers **smarter filters across the app**, **shared saved views for teams**, **clearer filter visibility**, **faster dashboard performance**, and **more predictable navigation**.

Below are the most important changes in this release (not every internal tweak, just the ones we think deliver the most value).

---

## 🔎 **Smarter filters that stay with you**

- **Filters now persist across key pages:** Your active filter now carries across Explorer, dashboards, and account or user pages instead of resetting as you move around the product — You can keep the same focused view of your data without repeating setup on each page.
- **Saved filters are quicker to access:** Saved filters are easier to turn on from the main navigation and faster to open in filtering menus — It takes less time to switch between focused views and broader data sets.
- **Filter switching feels immediate:** Changing the active filter in Explorer and Dashboard now updates the selected state right away, with clearer loading feedback while results refresh — You get faster confirmation that your selection is being applied and less uncertainty while waiting for data to update.

## 💾 **Saved views are easier to share and manage**

- **Filtered views can be saved and shared:** You can now save a filtered view and share it with your team — Teams can work from the same results and stay aligned without rebuilding filters individually.
- **Saved filters are simpler to manage:** Saved filters now have a more streamlined management experience, including options to rename, share, make private, or remove them in one place — It is easier to keep shared and personal views organized as your team’s needs change.
- **First-time setup is more guided:** New onboarding guidance helps newer users create their first filter more quickly — People can get to useful filtered views faster, even if they are new to the product.

## 🧭 **Clearer and more predictable filtering**

- **Active filters are visible in Explorer:** Explorer once again shows active filters as chips so you can see what is shaping your results at a glance — It is easier to understand why you are seeing a particular set of results.
- **Filter menus are easier to scan:** The filter menu has been cleaned up to reduce clutter and make saved views easier to browse — You can find and switch views more quickly.
- **Confusing filter options were removed:** Some filter options that could produce unclear or unreliable results are no longer shown — You are less likely to run into filtering paths that create confusion.
- **Navigation stays on track during filter changes:** Changing or clearing an active filter now keeps you in the right part of the product more reliably, and Explorer filtering behaves more predictably — You can stay focused on your current workflow instead of being redirected unexpectedly.

## ⚡ **Faster dashboards and smoother layouts**

- **Dashboard performance improved:** Dashboard pages now load faster, especially when filters are applied — Charts and tables respond with less waiting, making analysis feel more fluid.
- **Charts resize correctly with the sidebar:** Charts now adjust properly when the sidebar is opened or collapsed — Dashboard layouts stay readable as you move through the product.

## 🚧 **Better guidance where filtering is unavailable**

- **Unsupported pages now explain filter limits:** On Live View and Magic Importer pages, the filter control is disabled and the product explains why filtering is not available there — This prevents dead-end actions and makes product behavior easier to understand.

Thanks for using Brixo! We hope these updates make your day-to-day workflows smoother, faster, and more intuitive.
