This release delivers **more reliable search availability**, **less disruption during maintenance**, **more consistent performance under load**, and **fewer failure-related slowdowns**.

Below are the most important changes in this release (not every internal tweak, just the ones we think deliver the most value).

---

## 🔎 **More reliable search availability**

- **Resilience to single-server failures:** Search is better protected if an individual server has an issue, helping keep search results available and responsive — users are less likely to see outages or slowdowns from isolated problems.
- **Reduced shared points of failure:** Search services are distributed in a way that lowers the chance that multiple critical components are impacted at the same time — search stays up more consistently, even when parts of the underlying system are experiencing problems.

## 🛠️ **Less disruption during maintenance**

- **Fewer maintenance-related interruptions:** Planned maintenance and routine infrastructure changes are less likely to interrupt search availability — teams can schedule operational work with less risk of search downtime for end users.
- **Reduced performance dips during updates:** Additional safeguards reduce the chance of degraded search performance during updates or operational changes — search remains steadier during change windows, with fewer periods of slow or inconsistent results.

## ⚙️ **More consistent performance under load**

- **Dedicated capacity for search:** More capacity is reserved specifically for search to improve consistency under heavier usage and reduce resource contention — search performance is more predictable during peak demand, with fewer slowdowns when usage spikes.
- **Overall dependability improvements:** This release focuses on reliability rather than user-visible feature changes, aiming to make search more dependable day to day — customers should experience fewer incidents and a more stable search experience without changing workflows.

Thanks for using Brixo! We hope these updates make your day-to-day workflows smoother, faster, and more intuitive.
