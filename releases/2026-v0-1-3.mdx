This release delivers **more reliable activity capture**, **steadier performance during busy periods**, **fewer save failures**, and **faster recovery from slowdowns**.

Below are the most important changes in this release (not every internal tweak, just the ones we think deliver the most value).

---

## 🛡️ **More reliable activity capture**

- **Safer handling of high-volume updates:** Incoming activity is processed more safely when many updates arrive at once — Reduces delays and failed saves during peak usage.

## ⚡ **Improved stability as usage grows**

- **Fewer background processing bottlenecks:** Background processing was optimized to reduce bottlenecks that build up under load — Keeps your workspace steadier and more predictable as activity increases.

## 🚦 **Quicker recovery from slow work**

- **Earlier stop for slow or stuck jobs:** Unusually slow or stuck work is now stopped sooner instead of tying up resources — Helps the app stay responsive and avoids cascading slowdowns.

Thanks for using Brixo! We hope these updates make your day-to-day workflows smoother, faster, and more intuitive.
