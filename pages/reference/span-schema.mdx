- `gen_ai.prompt.*`, `gen_ai.completion.*`
- `gen_ai.usage.prompt_tokens`, `gen_ai.usage.completion_tokens`, `gen_ai.usage.total_tokens`

## HTTP/tool spans
- `http.method`, `http.url`, `http.status_code`
