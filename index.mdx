## Why instrument with Brixo?
- **Frictionless integration**: send raw OTEL spans or point your OpenAI calls to our proxy.
- **Readable step timelines**: we infer steps automatically; optionally add semantic spans via Traceloop/OpenLLMetry.
- **Business‑grade analytics**: tasks, steps, outcomes, sentiment, cost, latency and more. Check out our [Demo Agent Analytics Dashboard](https://www.brixo.com/agentdemo).

## Choose your path
1. **Zero‑code (HTTP)** · Use the **OpenAI Proxy** for files/batches/requests. → [/http/openai-proxy](/http/openai-proxy)
2. **Auto‑instrument (OTEL)** · Keep your SDK; emit OpenAI/HTTP/tool spans. → [/ingestion/overview](/ingestion/overview)
3. **Optional semantic steps** · Add Traceloop/OpenLLMetry to label workflows/tasks. → [/ingestion/frameworks](/ingestion/frameworks)

> You can combine 2 + 3: raw spans for depth, semantic steps for a readable story.
